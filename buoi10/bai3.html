ARTORIA PENDRAGON
Time appears: Late 5th century early 6th century

5 things to know about artoria pendragon

- The legend originates from the fact that Uther Pendragon (the current king of the United Kingdom) fell deeply in love with Igrayne (wife of Duke Gorlois of Cornwall) but was rejected by her because he feared it would interfere with the affairs of the two countries. Being rejected caused Uther to fall ill and had to summon Merlin (a magician born to a woman and an Incubus) to cure him. Merlin helped Uther transform into Gorlois (Igrayne's husband) so that Uther could have sex with the woman of his dreams. Some time later, Igrayne became pregnant and gave birth to Arthur Pendragon. But fearing that it would cause feud between the two kingdoms, Uther ordered Merlin to give the baby to Ector and his wife to take care of.
